Welcome to the Incidents Management App

Your Trusted Tool for Reporting and Managing Incidents

At Experion, your safety, security, and well-being are our top priorities. We understand that incidents can happen anytime, and it's crucial to address them promptly and efficiently. That's why we've created the Incidents Management App – a user-friendly platform where you can report incidents quickly, track their progress, and ensure they are resolved in a timely manner.

Why Use Our App?

  • Ease of Reporting: Whether it's a security breach, a quality issue, or a privacy concern, you can report incidents directly from your device, no matter where you are.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Stay informed about the status of your reported incidents. Know when your issue is being reviewed, addressed, and resolved.
  • Transparency: Keep track of how incidents are handled. Get notified about the steps taken by the administration to resolve your concerns.

Getting Started: App Walkthrough

Step 1: Awareness Page

When you first log in to the app, you'll land on this page. Here, you can learn about the importance of reporting incidents and how you can contribute to creating a safer and more secure workplace.

Step 2: Dashboard Screen

Report an Incident: This is the primary feature of our app. Click on the 'Report Incident' button to start reporting any security, quality, or privacy-related incident. The form is simple and intuitive.

Track Progress: Once you report an incident, you can track its status in real-time. See when it’s assigned, in progress, or resolved.

Step 3: Incident Reporting

Choose the Incident Type: Select the type of incident you are reporting – Security, Quality, or Privacy.

Provide Details: Fill in the required details such as date, time, and description. Attach any relevant documents or images to support your report.

Submit: After reviewing the information, hit submit. Your report will be instantly logged, and the relevant team will be notified.

Step 4: Notifications

Stay Updated: Get real-time notifications about the status of your incident report. You’ll be notified when your report is reviewed, when any action is taken, and when the incident is resolved.
